Simple Licensing

One License. Unlimited Use.

We believe in simplicity. When you buy a template, it's yours to use on as many personal or commercial projects as you want.

What is Allowed

You are free to:

  • Use the template for unlimited personal and commercial projects.
  • Use the template for client work (freelance or agency).
  • Modify the source code to fit your specific needs.
  • Convert the HTML into other technologies (React, Vue, WordPress theme).

What is NOT Allowed

You are forbidden to:

  • Resell, redistribute, or license the template as-is.
  • Create a competing product (e.g., a "UI Kit" or "Theme") based on our code.
  • Share your license key or download link with others.
  • Open source the UI design itself (you can open source the logic, but not the assets).

Common Scenarios

Client Work

Yes! You can buy a template and use it to build a website for your client. You can charge your client for the website.

SaaS Product

Yes! You can use the template to build the dashboard or frontend of your SaaS application and charge users for access.

Open Source

No. You cannot use our template in a public repo where the source code is available to everyone for free.

Team Use

Yes! You can share the template files with other developers in your team working on the same project.
